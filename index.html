About Me

My name is Nick Volkert! I'm an artist and visual designer from the North Side of Chicago. I received my BFA in Illustration and MFA in Painting from NIU. You can see my work across the web on my dribbble page. When I'm not creating art, I'm studying code in hopes of becoming a Full Stack Developer. In my freetime I enjoy checking shows in the Chicago music scene, lifting weights, drawing comics, watching Chicago Bears games and rooting for the Chicago White Sox.
